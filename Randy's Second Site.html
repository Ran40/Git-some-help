-First things first, you're gonna need ice cream. Obviously, it's what milkshakes are built upon. Now, any ice cream will do as long as it's Personally, I enjoy using vanilla ice cream (which is trash by itself, by the way). About 7 scoops is what I prefer.

-Next, take whatever it is you need to make it a certain flavor and add it. I have a peanut butter milkshake in mind--which you obviously don't want if you're allergic, so again, use whatever you want--so 3 spoonfuls will do.

-Then, add some milk for your desired thickness. I usually add one liter of milk since I don't like when milkshakes are very thick, but make sure you add enough so that your ingredients mix well and that you don't end up breaking your blender.... ....Now hit blend!

-Finally, once mixed to your satisfaction, taste it. If it's not doing it for you, add some more ice cream, your "whatever", or both.

-And with that, you're done! Hopefully, this was a simple, fun process for you. Enjoy your shake!
